Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people because it gives them hope that it is possible to become smarter by repeated practice.

Discuss how you might engage with the principals of neuroplasticity for your own benefit

For my own benefit, I can improve my intelligence by repeatedly doing activities that are fundamental, such as reading, memorizing and thinking ahead.

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity can be increased by learning something new, like a new language or dance, which strengthens the brain.

Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

The growth mindset is about our ability to increase our intelligence by putting more effort into things we want to be better in. This mindset is relevant to our success in life as we are able to persevere through hard challenges that others with a fixed mindset might not be able to handle.
