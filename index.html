About Me

My Occupation

I am a full time student studying Computer Systems Engineering at the University of Auckland. In this degree, we study about how electronic circuits function and how to program computers at the level close to the hardware.

My Passion

I have a passion for technology, problem solving and design, which lead me to take Computer Systems at university and is why I learn web development in my spare time.

My Practices

When I design something, it must be something I can be proud of. I believe that even the little details shouldn't be ignored. When this comes to web development, I ensure my websites are responsive and attractive with fast loading times to give the best user experience. When it comes to programming in general, I ensure my code is readable, reusable and DRY.
